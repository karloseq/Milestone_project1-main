About

I created this website to share information about my sports progress. When I was 19, I ran a 5K in 15'38" and a 10K in under 32', but then stopped as professional training takes massive time and sacrifice. In the past decade, I realized I was missing adrenaline and challenges, and I wanted to improve my health as well. I hope that this content motivates others as well.
